About us

In 1993, the first Fitness First club opened in Bournemouth in the South Of England. Over the next decade and a half, Fitness first was present in over 16 countries, including India making it a leader in the global fitness industry. In 2018, Fitness First India war acquired by cure.fit, India's largest chain of fitness centres that offers energetic group workouts and multiple workout formats to choose from.

We are an ambitious company and we re always looking for great people to join our team from personal trainers, group exercise instructors and club managers to our front-of-house teams. Were committed to training, so you ll be encouraged to improve your existing skills while you develop new ones.
